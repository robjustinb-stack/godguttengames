📖 How to Play

🏝️ About You and your worst enemy Babbi have both been stranded on adjacent island groups. All you have is a raft and your wits. Between the islands is a helicopter that seats one person with only enough gas for one trip. Hidden on each island is a chest with a flight manual. Find the key, unlock the chest, and reach the helicopter before Babbi does.
🎯 Objective Find the key → unlock the chest → get the manual → reach the ★ tile to win.
⚙️ Setup Each player places their chest (land only), key, and mine on their island. Items are hidden from your opponent. Players start adjacent to the star on their opponent's island, and play takes place on the opposing player's island.
🎲 On Your Turn Choose one: Roll the die and spend action points on movement and digging in any order. Or ask Babbi a yes/no question about item locations (max 5 questions, uses the whole turn).
🚤 Movement Move horizontally or vertically only. Use the boat to cross water — boarding and disembarking each cost 1 action point. You cannot swim.
💣 Mine If you land on the mine it detonates — you miss your next turn and the mine is removed permanently.
🤥 The Lie Each player may lie once when answering a question. Once used all future answers are enforced as truthful.
❓ Questions Choose from a structured list. Max 5 questions per player. No repeated questions allowed.
