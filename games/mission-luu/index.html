About the Game

Mission: LUU is a cooperative card game for 1–2 players. You are astronaut scientists on an alien planet, cultivating single-cell Luu creatures and harvesting their clean energy secretion — Luutex — as your resource. Parasite-infected Rogue Luu have overrun five sectors. Fight through each one, defeat the sector boss, and win.


Before each game, draft your 40-card shared deck collaboratively from a pool of Core and Power cards. Add up to 5 Chaos Cards to raise the difficulty. Luu level up, evolve, and gain passive traits. Every run is different.
